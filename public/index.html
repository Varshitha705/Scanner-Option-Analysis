Options Entry & Alert Analysis

1. Alert Level = "Get Ready" Zone

Set alert 0.2%–0.5% below your breakout level.

Example: Resistance = $100.50
Alert = $100.30

This gives time to check volume and MACD before entering.

2. Entry Level = "Go" Trigger

All 3 conditions must occur together:

  • Price: 5M candle closes above resistance and VWAP
  • Volume: Volume greater than 2x average of previous 10 candles
  • MACD (8,17,9): MACD crosses above signal and histogram turns green

Enter after the candle closes to avoid fake breakouts.

3. Do NOT Enter When

  • Price is only above the 9/21 EMA
  • POC remains above current price
  • Green candle forms on weak volume

15-Minute Breakout Setup

  • Draw last 15M swing high + VWAP
  • Alert 1 = Breakout level -1%
  • Alert 2 = Volume spike greater than 2x average
  • Entry = 5M close above level + MACD confirmation

Options Tip

Wait 5–10 minutes after a breakout candle begins instead of buying during the wick. This can help avoid paying peak implied volatility.

Big candle chasing is risky. Theta decay and IV crush can reduce gains even when direction is correct. Always use stops and proper position sizing.
